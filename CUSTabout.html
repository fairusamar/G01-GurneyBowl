ABOUT US

GURNEY BOWL BOOKING SYSTEM

Gurney Bowl is a premier bowling alley located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur at Gurney Mall, Residensi UTMKL. We pride ourselves on offering state-of-the-art facilities and a fun, welcoming environment for all. Whether you're a student, staff member, or visitor, our affordable rates ensure that everyone can enjoy a fantastic bowling experience. Our friendly atmosphere and top-notch services make Gurney Bowl the perfect place to unwind, compete, and make lasting memories with friends and family.

DISCOVER OUR STORY

At Gurney Bowl, we are more than just a bowling alley. We host a wide range of events and promotions throughout the year, from exciting tournaments and competitive leagues to family fun nights and special discount days. Our vibrant community ensures there's always something happening, providing endless entertainment for bowlers of all ages and skill levels. Stay connected with us through our website and social media channels to catch the latest updates and join in on the fun.
